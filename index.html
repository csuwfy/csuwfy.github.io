Research

My interests are centered on models that connect perception with useful intermediate reasoning: medical reconstruction models that respect acquisition physics, and multimodal systems that can reason over spatial structure and action sequences.

Medical Imaging

Dynamic parallel MRI reconstruction, flow matching, spatiotemporal modeling, length extrapolation, data consistency, and uncertainty propagation.

Vision-Language Models

3D-aware representation learning, multi-step spatial reasoning, multimodal benchmarks, task decomposition, and self-reflective planning.

Embodied AI

Language-grounded manipulation, simulated robot execution, compact embedded autonomy, and systems that bridge visual reasoning with action.

Projects

A few representative projects from my CV. I kept this section text-first so the page stays credible and easy to scan.

Unsupervised Dynamic Parallel MRI Reconstruction
Sept 2025 - Nov 2026

Developing an unsupervised framework for accelerated cardiac MRI reconstruction using Projected Conditional Flow Matching and length-extrapolative spatiotemporal modeling.

  • Designed ST-Flow U-Net with factorized spatiotemporal modeling and ALiBi-augmented attention.
  • Evaluated generalization across multi-center and multi-scanner CMRxRecon datasets.
Vision-Language Models for Rubik's Cube Manipulation
Sept 2023 - Aug 2025

Built VLM-based systems for Rubik's cube manipulation and multi-step embodied reasoning, including CubeRobot, CubeCoT, Cube3D, and VisionCube.

  • Used task decomposition, self-reflection, and memory-guided replanning for action sequences.
  • Integrated 3D representation learning and simulated robot verification in RoboGuide.
MRI Reconstruction with Uncertainty Quantification
Sept 2025 - Present

Studying supervised flow matching for MRI reconstruction with uncertainty propagation through ODE integration and data consistency projection layers.

SDXL Manga Generator
Sept 2025 - Nov 2025

Built an end-to-end course project for audio-narrated multi-panel manga generation using story expansion, prompt engineering, LoRA-fine-tuned SDXL, identity preservation, inpainting, and TTS.
